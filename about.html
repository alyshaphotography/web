Hi, I’m Alysha.

I'm a portrait photographer here in beautiful Kamloops, British Columbia.

I love a good love story. I always cry at the end of Titanic, no matter how many times I've seen it. I also cried at Shrek. My point is – I'm emotional. I'm sensitive. I'm sentimental. I believe a photograph can send you back in time – to when you and your spouse were engaged, to when your teenagers were babies, back to the most important days of your life.

I live with my inspiring husband Caleb and our two birds and two cats. I would travel anywhere to have a soft pretzel. Every week I take photos for a youth group but more importantly I'm watching these teens grow into strong people. I am always ready to play Mario Kart and I'll stop whatever I'm doing to watch a sunset. Now I want to get to know you!
